About Me...

Born in the historical city of Den Bosch, ore you can call it Duketown as an outsider that has problems pronouncing it. From a young age i developed an interest in drawing and crafting creative things that are self imagined in my own imagination.

Later this translated into an affinity in painting, graphic design, art, photography and cinema photography. These interest are the diciplines I apply to this day and gave my own signature twist to it. Eventually I evolved my work mostly these days in photography. Photography that captures a moment beyond our imagination, but also inspiration from cinema, surrealism, and our society.
