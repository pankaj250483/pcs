WELCOME TO SINTRA PORTUGAL

Sitting just 14 miles outside of Lisbon, Sintra is a charming municipality on the Portuguese Riviera. The perfect destination for a winter's escapade, Sintra is known for its beautiful palaces and mansions, 19th-century gardens, and surrounding natural beauty. Visiting tourists can explore both Sintra's beaches and mountains in just one afternoon, but the town is best known for its lavish mountents and unique ambiance.
